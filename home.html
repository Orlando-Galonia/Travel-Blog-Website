LET'S GO

10 REASONS WHY IT'S MORE FUN IN THE PHILIPPINES

1. Discover the hidden waterfalls of Cebu, a perfect mix of adventure and serenity.

2. Relax on the pristine white sands of Boracay and watch the world fade away.

3. Conquer Mt. Pulag and catch the sea of clouds from the summit at dawn.

4. Immerse yourself in rich Filipino culture through vibrant festivals and heritage sites.

5. Dive into the colorful coral reefs of Tubbataha, one of the world's best dive sites.

6. Experience the serenity of a cruise along Loboc River in Bohol with local music onboard.

7. Trek to Sagada and marvel at the hanging coffins and the breathtaking landscapes.

8. Explore the historical streets of Vigan and walk through the Spanish colonial era.

9. Uncover the hidden lagoons and stunning beaches of Palawan, a paradise on Earth.

10. Experience the vibrant nightlife and city life in the heart of Manila.
